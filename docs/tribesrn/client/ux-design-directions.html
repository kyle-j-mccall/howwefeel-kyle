Clean, understated rings with subtle visual cues. Lets the contact card be the hero. Best for users who want focus without distraction.

Evaluation Criteria

🎯 Brand Alignment

Does it feel adventurous, warm, and playful?

👆 Interaction Clarity

Is it obvious how to flick to labels?

🧭 Navigation

Is domain switching intuitive?

😊 Emotional Response

Does it spark joy and nostalgia?

♿ Accessibility

Are targets large enough? Contrast sufficient?

🔄 Scalability

Will it work with 13+ labels per domain?
